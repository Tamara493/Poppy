Poppy

Poppy is a cute and playful puppy, who is a mix between a Loulou and a Bichon. She has the fluffy white coat and playful nature of a Bichon, and the friendly and affectionate personality of a Loulou. Poppy loves to play with toys and is always up for a game of fetch. She's also very social and enjoys spending time with her human family. Poppy is a great companion and a perfect addition to any household.

Poppy is a very affectionate puppy and has a strong bond with her human mom. She loves to follow her around the house and go everywhere her mom goes. She is also very playful and loves her toys, she has a collection of different toys such as stuffed animals, balls, and rope toys. Poppy is always eager to play and will often bring her toys to her mom to initiate a game. She is a very loyal and loving companion, and her mom is lucky to have such a wonderful puppy as Poppy.
